About InKing Art Studio

Who Are We?

We are a small creative business, specializing in body art, portraiture, identity branding and storefront signage.
Originally founded in 2018 by Josiah Bayne, InKing Art studio has made its mark as Enterprise’s premier graphic design and body art destination.
 
We believe in art,
 
We believe in creative expression,
 
We believe everyone has the right to appreciate it, on a device, on a wall, on a canvas and on skin.
 
InKing Art Studio is a place where artists and art lovers can come together, and we welcome anyone who feels inspired by what we do.

Want a tasmanian devil on your calf? We can do that.
But we’re going to walk you through all the options.

Want to rebrand your company from scratch? We can do that too, and you’re going to get the same attention and quality of work.
 
Art is our way of life. We live and breathe this passion,

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Sometimes the answers aren't always obvious. That's why we've compiled a list of the top most asked questions, and we're glad to help. Give us a call if yours didn't make the list!
 

HOW MUCH ARE PIERCINGS?

Basic piercings are $35. Dermal anchors start at $55. Genital piercings start at $60. We accept cash and credit card payments. We do have an ATM machine for your convenience.
 

HOW MUCH DOES A TATTOO COST?

The cost of your tattoo depends on a number of factors including Size, Placement, Skin Type, and Color vs. Black & Grey. For smaller tattoos, we will generally quote you a price upfront, and we have no shop minimum at Certified. For larger tattoos, your artist may opt to quote you an hourly rate (this is at each artist’s discretion). We accept cash payments only, but we do have an ATM machine for your convenience.
 

DO I NEED AN APPOINTMENT?

Walk-In's are always welcome! No appointment is necessary for smaller designs. However, if you’re interested in a larger tattoo, we suggest coming in and speaking with an artist ahead of time about your ideas. Appointments are suggested for anyone who wants a custom design. Your artist will take a non-refundable deposit before an appointment is set. Your cash deposit is applied toward the total cost of the tattoo. Deposits are non-refundable.
